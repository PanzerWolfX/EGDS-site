In the tumultuous year of 2035, amidst the clash of titans between the European Federation and the resurgent Ottoman Empire, EuroGuardian Defense Solutions (EGDS) forged a division like no other: TaevasHüpe Üksus.
Born from the crucible of conflict and steeped in secrecy, this autonomous division emerged as the silent guardian of EGDS's most closely guarded secrets.

s Specializing in the art of Atmo-jumping, TaevasHüpe Üksus possessed the unparalleled ability to deploy troops, vehicles, mechs, and vital resources directly from orbit to the heart of the battlefield.
Their arrival was as swift as lightning, striking fear into the hearts of their adversaries and turning the tide of battle in the European Federation's favor.

However, despite their formidable prowess, the war over Balkan independence ended in victory for the Ottoman Empire.
Yet, amidst the chaos of defeat, TaevasHüpe Üksus played a crucial role in concealing the existence of EGDS's futuristic space fleets and space stations.

Deployed covertly and sparingly throughout the conflict, TaevasHüpe Üksus operated in the shadows, their missions shrouded in secrecy to protect the Federation's most precious assets.
Unlike other divisions such as the Redemption Legion of Europa and the StahlEngel Divisions, whose actions were more overt, TaevasHüpe Üksus remained hidden from the prying eyes of both friend and foe alike.

As the dust settled and the echoes of war faded, TaevasHüpe Üksus faded into legend, their deeds known only to a select few within EGDS.
However, their legacy endured as a testament to the corporation's unwavering commitment to protecting humanity's future, even in the darkest of times. In the annals of history, TaevasHüpe Üksus would forever be remembered as the silent sentinels who safeguarded the Federation's secrets and ensured that hope endured in the face of adversity.
