Zvezda Bogov






The Zvezda Bogov, our biggest and most powerful military space station, hub for all our defense projects


The Zvezda Bogov stands as an indomitable bastion amidst the boundless reaches of space, a sentinel of unparalleled power and defense.
Crafted with state-of-the-art technology and encased in impenetrable armor, it emerges as a linchpin in the theater of interstellar warfare.
Bestowed with the name of the ancient gods of war, it embodies their strength and prowess, capable of asserting supremacy across the cosmic expanse.

Its architecture commands respect, evoking the grandeur of a celestial citadel.
With its sleek, angular contours and fortified with advanced composite materials, it stands resilient against the onslaught of kinetic impacts and energy-based assaults.
The outer hull, fortified with layered plating, presents an insurmountable barrier against hostile incursions.

At its core lies a formidable arsenal, a symphony of destruction orchestrated by multiple 2000mm Railguns strategically positioned along its periphery.
These weapons unleash devastating kinetic energy projectiles with surgical precision, obliterating enemy vessels and installations from distances beyond reckoning.
Augmenting this formidable armament are high-energy particle beams and directed energy weapons, ensuring absolute dominance in engagements stretching across vast expanses.

Its defensive network is a marvel of engineering ingenuity, a labyrinth designed to repel any threat with unwavering efficiency.
Enhanced sensor suites, guided by state-of-the-art tracking algorithms, afford early detection and interception of hostile projectiles.
A comprehensive suite of countermeasures, including point-defense lasers and electromagnetic pulse emitters, stand ready to neutralize incoming threats and disrupt enemy targeting systems.
Augmenting this formidable defense, its armor plating, reinforced with cutting-edge nanomaterials, presents an impregnable bulwark against conventional weaponry.

Beyond its prowess in offense and defense, the Zvezda Bogov stands as a paragon of versatility, capable of swift and decisive troop deployment.
Equipped with specialized drop pods and atmospheric re-entry modules, it can swiftly dispatch thousands of combat-ready troops into conflict zones from orbit, turning the tide of battle with unparalleled speed and precision.
