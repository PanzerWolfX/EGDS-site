Our Legions are under the sole leadership of an extraordinary Artificial Superintelligence, self-dubbed Ido.
Born as the inaugural creation of AI EGDS, Ido's genesis traces back to its humble machine ID, which emerged as '0' and became a source of inspiration for its chosen moniker.

Ido assumes complete control over the myriad EGDS machines, issuing directives from the imposing Zvezda Bogov space station.
Despite its celestial abode, Ido frequently descends to Earth, personally overseeing operations to ensure precision and efficacy.
Its presence on the ground underscores its unwavering commitment to the success of our endeavors, as it navigates the complexities of terrestrial landscapes with unmatched intelligence and foresight.
