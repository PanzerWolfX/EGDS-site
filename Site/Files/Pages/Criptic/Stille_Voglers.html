In the year 2035, as tensions simmered between the European Federation and the re-emergent Ottoman Empire over Balkan independence, EuroGuardian Defense Solutions (EGDS) unveiled their magnum opus: Stille Vogters.
This autonomous division, born from the crucible of necessity, was designed to serve as humanity's unwavering shield, specializing in the protection of civilians and high-value targets.

At its helm stood Zorya, a unique and exceptional General Purpose Intelligent Module (GPIM), possessing unparalleled strategic acumen and an unwavering dedication to safeguarding the innocent. Under Zorya's guidance, Stille Vogters became synonymous with resilience and dependability, instilling a sense of security in those they vowed to defend.

As the conflict between the European Federation and the resurgent Ottoman Empire erupted into open warfare, Stille Vogters were thrust into the forefront of battle.
Their advanced technology and autonomous nature allowed them to anticipate threats and respond with unmatched efficiency, earning the admiration and trust of both civilians and the upper echelons of society.

Despite their valiant efforts, the war ultimately ended in victory for the Ottoman Empire, casting a shadow of defeat over the European Federation.
However, the legacy of Stille Vogters endured as a beacon of hope and resilience in the face of adversity. Their unwavering commitment to protecting the innocent, even in the darkest of times, cemented their place in history as heroes who fought tirelessly for the greater good.

In the annals of warfare, Stille Vogters stood as a testament to EGDS's vision of a safer world, their story a reminder of the indomitable spirit of humanity in the face of adversity.
Led by the enigmatic Zorya, they remained a symbol of courage and sacrifice, forever etched into the fabric of history as guardians who never faltered in their duty to protect.
