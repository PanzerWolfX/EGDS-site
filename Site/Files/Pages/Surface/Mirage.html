EHB-03 Mirage

A Mirage inside its hangar



The European Heavy Bomber, codenamed EHB or Mirage, not to be confused with the omonym French independently developed fighter, represents a pinnacle in aerial warfare, boasting nuclear capabilities alongside its stealth design.

The EHB-003 is an imposing aircraft engineered for high-altitude, swift, and covert operations, capable of swiftly neutralizing significant threats undetected. With a staggering maximum speed of Mach 3.7 and an impressive operational range of approximately 10,000 kilometers, coupled with aerial refueling capabilities, it possesses unparalleled global strike capabilities.

Its armament suite is equally formidable, ranging from precision-guided munitions to heavy bombs.
Standard configuration includes two 1000 kg bombs and an anti-air missile for defensive purposes, ensuring versatility and adaptability in various mission scenarios.
