EGPIM-01




Like the Phalanxes of ancient Greeks and Romans, the General Purpose Intelligent Machines (GPIM) serve as the backbone of our United European Defense.
These machines, controlled by sophisticated Artificial Intelligence algorithms, are meticulously designed with robust fail-safes to prevent any potential misuse or hostile actions against us.

Equipped with intelligent systems capable of autonomous speech and thought, these machines are granted significant autonomy to maximize their efficiency and effectiveness on the battlefield.

The Military variant boasts extensive networking capabilities, akin to hive minds, enhancing coordination among armed forces and optimizing their effectiveness. Additionally, they can be outfitted with specialized upgrade packages tailored to different environments or combat scenarios.

Conversely, the Civilian variant is engineered to execute a wide array of tasks as needed, ensuring versatility and adaptability across various non-combat scenarios.
