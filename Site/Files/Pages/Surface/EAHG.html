A Kravi Orao in the hangar



The Krvavi Orao, a formidable marvel in aerial warfare, embodies the epitome of lethal precision and relentless combat prowess.
Designed with an emphasis on versatility and stealth, this gunship stands as a symbol of dominance in both close air support and anti-armor operations.

Equipped with a sleek and stealthy exterior, the Krvavi Orao strikes fear into the hearts of its adversaries before they even realize its presence.
Positioned on the sides of its fuselage, the gun pods bristle with rapid-fire cannons, ready to unleash a barrage of devastation upon enemy forces.
The front belly armament, boasting a formidable 100 mm chain gun, serves as a relentless force against ground targets, leaving destruction in its wake.
But the Krvavi Orao's lethality extends beyond conventional warfare. With air-to-air missiles (AAMs) and air-to-ground missiles (AGMs) lining its sides, it possesses the capability to engage threats from all directions with unparalleled precision.
Underneath its wings, rocket pods stand poised, ready to deliver swift and decisive strikes against fortified positions.

Within its cockpit, a pilot and a weapons systems officer operate in seamless coordination, aided by advanced heads-up displays (HUDs) that provide unparalleled situational awareness.
The interior, meticulously designed for comfort and efficiency, accommodates the crew and their mission-specific equipment, ensuring peak performance in the heat of battle.

As it prowls the skies, the Krvavi Orao embodies the essence of stealth and precision, striking fear into the hearts of its enemies while providing a shield of protection for friendly forces below.
With its name whispered in awe and fear across the battlefield, the Bloody Eagle soars as a harbinger of victory for those who wield its power.
